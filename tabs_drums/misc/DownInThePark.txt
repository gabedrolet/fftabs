Foo Fighters - Down In the Park (Drum)
(From the "X-Files Soundtrack" and "My Hero" single)

Note: Will used only ride cymbals (no crashes) so I'll refer to each cymbal in
the tab by size (20RC, 22RC, and 24RC to correspond w/ 20, 22, and 24 inch rides). 
The snare rolls are almost completely inaudible, but they're there. 

Hi-hat o = open
x = closed

Snare o = normal hit
r = snare roll
